Call of Duty Game mode Guides

Domination is an objective Game Mode in Call of Duty Black Ops III's Multiplayer. Two teams of four to six players face off against each other in a two round match. Capture and hold three objectives on the map to earn points on the scoreboard. The first team to reach 100 points will win the first round. The next team to reach 200 points in the second round will win the game. The points from the first round will carry over to the second. Team Deathmatch is a death match Game Mode in Call of Duty Black Ops III's Multiplayer -- as the name implies. Two teams of four to six players face off against each other in a match to the death. The first team to reach 100 kills within the 10 minute time limit will win. If the time runs out, the team with the most kills wins Hardpoint is an objective Game Mode in Call of Duty Black Ops III's Multiplayer similar to the classic King of the Hill. Two teams of four to six players face off against each other in this fast-paced match. Fight over specified Hardpoint locations to earn points for the team. The first team to reach 250 points within the 5 minute time limit will win the game. Each Hardpoint will last for 60 seconds before moving to a new location on the map. One point will be earned per second is scored when a Hardpoint is secured. If an enemy player enters the Hardpoint, the area will be contested and no points will be earned
